About Sakura Proxy

What is TIXU Proxy?

TIXU Proxy is a free web proxy service that allows you to browse the internet anonymously and securely. Our servers located in Japan provide fast and reliable access to websites while protecting your privacy.

How it works

When you visit a website through Sakura Proxy, your connection is encrypted and routed through our servers. The website sees our server's IP address instead of yours, keeping your identity and location private.
